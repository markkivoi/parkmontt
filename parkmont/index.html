About Us


Parkmont came into being as a sister company to Shaun & Shane Associates LLC based in the United States to extend professional service to the African continent by leveraging global experience / expertise customized to country and region specific.

Since its inception, Parkmont has provided strategic services to enhance clients’ ability to realize their goals and objectives by providing solution-oriented expertise taking into consideration a dynamic global business environment. Parkmont’s success is based on its competitive market analysis, feasibility study, recognizing changing business landscape and clients demands thereby leverage its global expertise using highly skilled pool of talent.

It is our earnest purpose and plan to work closely with our clients identify challenges and provide practical solutions that produce desired results.
